Power consumption is linearly dependent on the frequency and squared dependent (if that's correct way of phrasing it) on voltage. In other words cutting voltage in 2 results in 4 times lower power consumption while cutting frequency in 2 will cut the power consumption in 2. ``` power_consumption = some_random_constant * frequency * voltage * voltage; ```
