auto-cpufreq is looking for co-maintainers & open source developers to help shape future of the project!

Discussion #312

Description

_Update: If you would like to discuss anything regardin auto-cpufreq or its development, please join [auto-cpufreq Discord server!](https://discord.gg/jYvWNr68)_ At the time of writing this, auto-cpufreq has 2243 stars on Github and with such exponential growth it's hard to keep up with everything. Especially since I do its development and everything else that requires running one such project in my free time without any financial compensation. I announced I was looking for co-maintainers after project reached over 1000 stars on both [Twitter](https://twitter.com/fooctrl/status/1400141578937720835) and [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/adnanhodzic_github-adnanhodzicauto-cpufreq-automatic-activity-6805907547158007808-Ay3F/) but it didn't get a lot of traction and no one reached out. Considering project is open source over the time it had a [lot of contributors](https://github.com/AdnanHodzic/auto-cpufreq/graphs/contributors), which have all been [credited for their work](https://github.com/AdnanHodzic/auto-cpufreq/releases) but no one stuck around. Linux is constantly evolving space and there are always new [issues and feature requests](https://github.com/AdnanHodzic/auto-cpufreq/issues), and since I encourage [contributing to the project,](https://github.com/AdnanHodzic/auto-cpufreq/#code-contribution) there's a constant influx of [PR's to review](https://github.com/AdnanHodzic/auto-cpufreq/pulls). Hence I'm looking for (long term) co-maintainer/s and developers who would help with workload of project going forward. I will not leave the project and will stay in its lead. I will still help with the workload and help onboard new members and together I would like us to shape future of the project. In its current form auto-cpufreq is very stable and besides implementing [existing feature requests](https://github.com/AdnanHodzic/auto-cpufreq/issues?q=is%3Aissue+feature+is%3Aopen), next big feature I would need help with is to have it implemented as a [GNOME extension](https://extensions.gnome.org/). Please let me know if you're interested in improving Linux and helping the overall Open Source community by being part of auto-cpufreq! Adnan
